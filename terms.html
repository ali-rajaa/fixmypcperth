What you need to know as our customer

These terms apply to all services provided by FixMyPC Perth, Western Australia. By booking a service you agree to the terms below.

1
Services

FixMyPC Perth provides computer repair, diagnostics, hardware upgrades, virus removal, data recovery, networking, and related technology services to residential and small business customers across Perth, Western Australia.

All services are carried out by our own team. We do not subcontract your device to a third party.

We reserve the right to decline a job if the repair is beyond our scope or if we determine the cost of repair is not in the customer's best interest. In that case we will tell you and not charge you beyond the diagnostic fee.

2
Diagnostic Fee and Pricing

A flat diagnostic fee applies to all drop off repair jobs. This fee covers a full assessment of your device, identification of the root cause, and the repair itself where possible.

The diagnostic fee is quoted to you before any work begins. You will always know the cost upfront and no additional labour charges are added without your approval.

If parts are required (such as an SSD, RAM, or screen), these are quoted separately. Parts costs are confirmed with you before anything is ordered or installed.

No surprises policy: If the repair turns out to be more complex than initially assessed, we will stop, explain the situation, and give you a revised quote before continuing. You are never committed to proceeding.
3
Payment

Payment is due on collection of your device, once the repair is complete and you are satisfied with the work. No upfront payment is required.

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Cash (exact change appreciated)
  • Bank transfer (BSB and account number provided on your invoice)

An invoice is provided for every job with a full breakdown of what was completed. Please allow up to 24 hours for bank transfers to clear before collecting your device if paying remotely in advance.

We do not currently accept card payments, PayPal, or buy now pay later services.

4
Warranty

All labour carried out by FixMyPC Perth comes with a 30 day labour warranty. If the same issue returns within 30 days of your repair being completed, we will reassess and remedy it at no additional labour cost.

The warranty covers the specific fault that was repaired. It does not cover:

  • New or unrelated faults that develop after the repair
  • Physical damage caused after collection (drops, spills, etc.)
  • Issues caused by software the customer installs after the repair
  • Faults caused by power surges or external events

Hardware components such as SSDs and RAM carry their own manufacturer warranty in addition to our labour warranty.

To claim the warranty simply message us on WhatsApp within 30 days describing the issue. We will arrange a time to reassess at no cost to you.
5
Your Data and Privacy

We understand that your device contains personal information. We take your privacy seriously and operate with the following principles:

  • We only access files, folders, or accounts that are necessary to complete the repair
  • For certain repairs (such as software issues, virus removal, or Windows reinstalls), we may require your device password or login credentials to carry out the work. This information is used solely for the repair and is never stored or shared
  • We do not browse, copy, or retain any personal files, photos, documents, or emails beyond what is necessary for the job
  • We do not sell or share your personal information with any third party
  • Contact details you provide (name, phone, email) are used only to communicate with you about your repair

We strongly recommend backing up your important data before handing in your device. While we take every precaution to protect your files, FixMyPC Perth cannot be held liable for pre-existing data loss or corruption discovered during the repair process.

Windows reinstalls: Before any reinstall, we back up your personal files and confirm with you exactly what will be retained. Nothing is wiped without your explicit approval.
6
Data Backup and Liability

FixMyPC Perth takes all reasonable care when handling your device. However, computer repair inherently carries some risk, particularly when a device is already in a failed or failing state.

We strongly advise all customers to back up their data before dropping off a device. If you are unsure how to do this, please let us know and we can assist.

FixMyPC Perth is not liable for:

  • Data loss resulting from a pre-existing failed or failing drive
  • Data loss where the customer was advised to back up before the repair
  • Damage to devices that were already structurally compromised at drop off
  • Loss of data on devices left uncollected beyond the 60 day collection period

In the unlikely event that our work causes unintended damage, we will discuss fair resolution with you directly.

7
Uncollected Devices

Once your repair is complete, we will notify you via WhatsApp or phone. We ask that you collect your device within a reasonable timeframe.

If a device has not been collected within 60 days of the repair completion notice, and we have been unable to reach you after reasonable attempts, FixMyPC Perth reserves the right to dispose of the device responsibly. This may include recycling, donation, or other lawful disposal.

We will always make multiple contact attempts before taking any action on an uncollected device.

Need more time? No problem at all. Just send us a message and we will hold your device for as long as needed. We just ask that you keep in touch.

We ask that a safe and accessible workspace is available for on site repairs.

Remote Support

Remote sessions are only conducted with the customer present and in control of ending the connection at any time.
