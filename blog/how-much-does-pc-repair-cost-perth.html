What Influences Laptop Repair Costs

While software fixes are usually fixed price, hardware repairs like laptop screen replacement depend on several factors:

  • Part Availability: Common models like Dell Inspiron or HP Pavilion have cheaper, readily available parts. MacBooks or Microsoft Surface devices require premium components.
  • Complexity: Replacing a keyboard on an old Dell takes 10 minutes. On a modern MacBook, it requires removing every internal component.
  • Urgency: Some shops charge a "rush fee" for same day service. We try to avoid this at FixMyPC Perth.
Pro tip: Always check if your technician offers a "No Fix, No Fee" guarantee. This means you only pay if the repair works, not if they try and fail.

Workshop Repairs vs Onsite Visits

Many Perth services offer onsite computer repair where they come to your home or office. While convenient, this is often the most expensive option because you're paying for travel time.

Common Questions About PC Repair Costs

Is it worth fixing a 5 year old laptop?

In most cases, absolutely. A 5 year old laptop with a slow hard drive can perform like new with an SSD upgrade for around $180 (parts and labour included). That's much cheaper than buying a new machine for $1,200.

Do you charge for diagnostics?

Many Perth shops charge $50 to $80 just to look at your computer. At FixMyPC Perth, we offer free basic assessments. If we can see the problem immediately, we won't charge you to tell you what it is.

How fast can you repair my PC?

80% of our repairs in Canning Vale are completed within 24 hours. Hardware parts that need to be ordered from interstate can add 2 to 3 days to the timeline.

Finding the Right Local Repairer

Price isn't everything. Reliability matters. When comparing Perth computer repair services, look for someone with local reviews, a clear warranty policy, and a fixed price approach. Stop paying by the hour and start paying for results.
