Common questions

Pricing questions, answered.

Everything people ask before booking a repair in Perth.

How much does computer repair cost in Perth?
Our all-in repair fee is $120, which covers the full diagnostic, all labour to fix the problem and a 30 day warranty. Individual repairs range from $79 for a password reset up to $249 for advanced data recovery from a completely dead drive. Every price on this page is what you actually pay, with GST included and no surprise fees added at the end.
Do I pay if you cannot fix my computer?
For data recovery from a completely dead drive, we operate on a strict no fix, no fee basis. If we cannot recover your files, you pay nothing at all. For general repairs, we will always tell you the likely outcome before we start so there are never any surprises. You approve the work before we touch anything.
How much does virus removal cost in Perth?
Virus and malware removal is $99 at FixMyPC Perth. This is a complete service covering deep scanning, full removal of all threats, browser cleanup, extension audit and security configuration to prevent reinfection. Most infections are fully resolved the same day you drop off the machine.
How much does an SSD upgrade cost in Perth?
If you supply your own SSD, the labour to fit it and transfer all your data is $65. If you want us to supply the drive, a 500GB Samsung or Crucial SSD fitted with full data transfer is $150, and a 1TB version is $185. An SSD upgrade is the single most effective upgrade you can make to a slow computer.
How much does data recovery cost in Perth?
Recovery from a failing, slow or clicking drive starts from $149. Recovery from a completely dead drive that will not spin or mount is $249 on a no fix no fee basis. If your drive is making strange noises or performing badly, stop using it immediately and contact us, as continued use reduces the chances of successful recovery.
How much does a gaming PC build cost in Perth?
Full custom gaming PC assembly using your own parts is $120 for labour. This includes everything from physical assembly and cable management through to driver installation and benchmark testing before handover. Individual upgrades like fitting a new GPU start from $60. We also offer PBO tuning and RGB sync as separate add-ons.
Is there a warranty on the repair work?
Yes. Every repair comes with a 30 day labour warranty starting from the day you collect your machine. If the same issue returns within 30 days, we fix it again at no charge. This is included in every service and is not an optional extra.
Can you come to me, or do I need to drop off?
Drop off at our Canning Vale location is the standard for most repairs and upgrades. For networking, printer setup, smart TV configuration and some software work, we come to you across southern Perth with a $40 callout fee. Remote support via AnyDesk is also available same day for software-only issues, Australia-wide.
