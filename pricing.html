Common PC and Laptop Problems We Fix in Perth

If your computer is showing any of the signs below, do not ignore them. Many issues that seem catastrophic are straightforward to resolve when caught early, and affordable to sort out at FixMyPC Perth with our transparent flat-rate pricing.

Slow computer that takes forever to load

A PC that crawls is almost always caused by one of three things: an ageing hard drive, too little RAM, or software clogging up startup. An SSD upgrade or a Windows optimisation service usually transforms the experience within an hour. Many Perth customers are amazed how fast their five-year-old laptop can feel after a $65 labour SSD swap.

Random shutdowns and blue screen errors

Blue screens and unexpected restarts are your computer's way of telling you something needs attention. Common causes include failing RAM, overheating, driver conflicts or a corrupted Windows installation. All of these are fixable, and our flat $99 repair fee covers the full diagnostic and all the labour to sort it.

Viruses, pop-ups and browser hijacking

If your browser is redirecting to strange sites, showing non-stop pop-up ads or your antivirus has disappeared, you almost certainly have a malware infection. Our $99 virus removal service eliminates threats completely and sets up protection against reinfection, with most infections cleared the same day you drop off in Canning Vale.

Computer won't turn on or boot past the logo

A PC or laptop that won't power on, gets stuck on the manufacturer logo or loops endlessly through restarts needs a proper hardware and software diagnostic. We identify the exact fault before quoting any repair, so you always know what you are paying for and why before we proceed.

Laptop overheating and fan running loud

Laptops are particularly prone to heat issues as dust accumulates over the years and thermal paste dries out. If your fan runs constantly at full speed or your laptop shuts off under load, a deep clean and thermal paste replacement almost always resolves it and extends the life of the machine significantly.

Broken laptop screen or keyboard

A cracked screen or a keyboard with dead keys does not mean you need a new laptop. We replace screens and keyboards on most major laptop brands at competitive labour rates, with parts quoted separately once we confirm your model. In most cases it costs less than a third of the price of a replacement machine.
