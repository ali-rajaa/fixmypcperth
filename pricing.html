Common questions

Pricing questions, answered.

Everything people ask before booking a repair in Perth.

How much does computer repair cost in Perth?
Our all-in repair fee is $120, covering the full diagnostic, all labour and a 30 day warranty. Repairs range from $79 for a password reset up to $249 for dead drive data recovery. All prices include GST with no hidden fees added at the end.
Do I pay if you cannot fix my computer?
For dead drive data recovery we operate no fix no fee. If we cannot recover your files, you pay nothing. For general repairs we tell you the likely outcome before starting, so there are never any surprises.
How much does virus removal cost in Perth?
Virus and malware removal is $99. This covers deep scanning, full threat removal, browser cleanup and security setup to prevent reinfection. Most infections cleared the same day you drop off.
How much does an SSD upgrade cost in Perth?
Labour only if you supply the SSD is $65. A 500GB Samsung or Crucial SSD supplied, fitted and data transferred is $150. A 1TB version is $185. An SSD upgrade is the single best upgrade for any slow computer.
How much does data recovery cost in Perth?
Recovery from a failing drive starts from $149. A completely dead drive that won't spin or mount is $249 on a no fix no fee basis. If your drive is clicking or making unusual sounds, stop using it immediately.
Is there a warranty on all repairs?
Yes. Every repair comes with a 30 day labour warranty from the day you collect your machine. If the same issue returns within 30 days, we fix it again at no charge. This is included in every service, not an optional extra.
How much does a gaming PC build cost in Perth?
Full custom gaming PC assembly from your parts is $120 for labour. This includes full assembly, cable management, driver installation and benchmark testing. GPU installation starts from $60 and PBO tuning is $60.
Can you come to me or do I drop off?
Drop off at our Canning Vale location is standard for repairs and upgrades. For networking, printer and smart TV work we come to you across southern Perth with a $40 callout fee. Remote support via AnyDesk is available same day for software issues, Australia-wide.
