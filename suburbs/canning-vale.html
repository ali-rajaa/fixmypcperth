Common Computer Problems We Fix for Canning Vale Residents

Canning Vale is one of Perth's largest southern suburbs. It is home to thousands of families, home-based businesses, tradies, and professionals who rely on their computers every day. When something goes wrong, you should not have to drive to Cannington or the city and wait a week. We are right here in Canning Vale and we fix these problems every day.

Computer Running Extremely Slow
Takes five minutes to open Chrome, freezes constantly, or feels slower every week. Usually caused by malware, a full or failing hard drive, or too little RAM for current Windows demands.
Virus, Pop-Ups or Browser Hijacked
Constant pop-up ads, homepage changed, searches redirected, or a security warning that will not close. Your machine almost certainly has malware and needs a professional clean.
Computer Will Not Turn On
Pressing the power button does nothing, screen stays black, or the machine starts and immediately shuts off. Could be a power supply, battery, motherboard, or overheating issue.
Laptop Screen Cracked or Broken
Cracked screen, lines running through the display, half the screen dark, or backlight completely gone. Most laptop screens can be replaced same-day with the right part in stock.
Files Lost or Drive Not Detected
Accidentally deleted photos, drive showing as unformatted, or a hard drive that is clicking and not spinning up. Data recovery with a no fix, no fee guarantee from $120.
Windows Crashing or Blue Screen Errors
Frequent blue screens, error messages at startup, or Windows stuck in a repair loop. Often caused by a failing drive, corrupted system files, or a bad Windows update.
Computer Overheating or Fan Very Loud
Fan running constantly at full speed, laptop burning hot to touch, or machine shutting down from heat. Dust buildup inside the case blocks airflow and is one of the most common repair jobs we see in Canning Vale homes.
Forgotten Password or Locked Out of Windows
Cannot get into your own computer after a Windows update changed your login, a PIN stopped working, or a Microsoft account is no longer accessible. We recover access the same day.
