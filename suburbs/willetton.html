Computer Running Extremely Slow
Takes five minutes to open a browser, freezes mid-task, or gets slower every week. Usually caused by malware, a full or failing hard drive, or not enough RAM for current Windows demands.
Virus, Pop-Ups or Browser Hijacked
Constant ads, homepage changed, searches redirected, or a security warning that locks your screen. Your machine almost certainly has malware and needs a professional clean.
Computer Will Not Turn On
Pressing the power button does nothing, screen stays black, or the machine shuts off immediately after starting. Could be a power supply, battery, motherboard or overheating issue.
Laptop Screen Cracked or Dead
Cracked panel, lines through the display, half the screen dark, or the backlight gone completely. Most laptop screens can be replaced same-day with the right part in stock.
Files Lost or Drive Not Detected
Accidentally deleted photos, a drive showing as unformatted, or a hard drive that is clicking. Data recovery with a no fix, no fee guarantee starting from $120.
Windows Crashing or Blue Screens
Frequent blue screen errors, error messages at startup, or Windows stuck in a repair loop. Often caused by a failing drive, corrupted system files, or a bad Windows update.
Computer Overheating or Fan Very Loud
Fan running at full speed constantly, laptop burning hot, or the machine shutting down from heat. Dust buildup inside the case is one of the most common repair jobs we see from Willetton homes.
Locked Out of Windows
Cannot get into your own computer after a Windows update, a PIN stopped working, or a Microsoft account is no longer accessible. We recover access same day.
