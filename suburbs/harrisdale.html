The City of Cockburn is one of Perth's fastest-growing local government areas. What they all have in common is a daily dependence on computers — for work, study, running a business, or staying connected. When a PC or laptop fails, the disruption is immediate.

We are based in Canning Vale, about 10 minutes from Cockburn Central, and we turn most repairs around the same day. Below are the problems we see most often from Cockburn households.

Computer Unbearably Slow
Waiting minutes for a browser to open, programs freezing mid-task, or the machine getting progressively worse over time. Common across all Cockburn suburbs and usually fixable without replacing the computer.
Virus, Malware or Ransomware
Pop-up ads that won't stop, a browser homepage you didn't set, searches redirecting to unknown sites, or a full lockout screen demanding payment. Cockburn residents near busy commercial areas are frequent targets for phishing and drive-by malware.
Computer Will Not Power On
Pressing the power button produces nothing — no fan, no light, no screen. Or the machine starts and immediately shuts down. Could be a power supply fault, failed battery, overheating, or a motherboard issue. We diagnose for free.
Cracked or Broken Laptop Screen
Cracked after a drop, lines running through the display, a section of the screen gone dark, or the backlight failed completely. Most laptop screens — including popular models used by Cockburn students and remote workers — can be replaced same-day.
Lost Files or Failed Hard Drive
Files accidentally deleted, a hard drive clicking or not detected, or a PC that died and took years of photos with it. We offer data recovery with a no fix, no fee guarantee from $120. Free assessment on drop-off.
Blue Screens and Windows Errors
Random blue screen crashes, error codes at startup, or Windows stuck in an automatic repair loop. Often caused by a dying hard drive, bad Windows update, or corrupted system files. We diagnose the root cause before recommending a fix.
Overheating and Fan Noise
Fan running loudly at all times, laptop too hot to use on your lap, or the machine shutting itself off after a short time. In Cockburn's warmer climate, dust-clogged cooling systems are a very common problem. A thermal clean usually resolves it same-day.
Locked Out of Your Own PC
Windows PIN no longer working, a Microsoft account you cannot access, or a computer that boots to a login you cannot get past. This happens frequently after forced Windows updates. We restore access the same day.
