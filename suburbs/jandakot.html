Computer Running Impossibly Slow
Opening a browser tab takes two minutes. Programs freeze mid-task. The machine crawls through things it used to handle instantly. In Jandakot's established homes, many desktops and laptops are still running ageing spinning hard drives — an SSD upgrade is often a complete transformation.
Virus, Malware or Ransomware
Browsers hijacked, pop-ups that won't stop, a lockout screen demanding payment — or the quieter kind where someone has access to your files and you don't yet know it. With Jandakot businesses running sensitive client data, professional documents and financial records, this is one we take seriously.
PC Won't Power On
No fan, no lights, no response when you press the button — or the machine starts and immediately shuts down. Could be a dead power supply, a failed battery, overheating that has tripped a safety cutoff, or a motherboard issue. We diagnose for free before recommending anything.
Cracked or Failed Laptop Screen
Dropped on a hard floor, a hairline crack that spread overnight, a screen that's dark on one side, or a backlight that has completely failed. Most popular laptop models can be repaired the same day with quality replacement panels. We check stock before you drive over.
Lost Files or Failing Hard Drive
A clicking or undetected drive, files that have vanished, or a laptop that died and took years of family photos with it. Data recovery with a no fix, no fee guarantee from $120 — we assess for free on drop-off and tell you exactly what's recoverable before you commit to anything.
Blue Screens and Windows Errors
Random BSOD crashes, a PC stuck in an endless Windows repair loop, or error codes that appear and disappear. Often a symptom of a failing drive or a corrupted Windows update — we find the root cause before recommending a fix, rather than guessing and hoping.
Overheating and Constant Fan Noise
A laptop too hot to use on your lap, a desktop fan spinning loudly all the time, or a machine that shuts itself off after 20 minutes. In Jandakot's warm summer climate, dust-clogged heatsinks and dried-out thermal paste are extremely common. A deep thermal clean usually solves it same-day.
